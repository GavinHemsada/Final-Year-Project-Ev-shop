## 1. General / Public User
*Accessible to anyone without logging in.*

### 1.1 Browsing the Platform
- **Home Page**: View featured electric vehicles, promotional banners, and core services.
- **Models Page**: Browse the full catalog of EVs. Use the search bar to find specific models (e.g., "Tesla").
- **Services**: Explore value-added services like Charging Station locator estimates or Repair costs.

### 1.2 Contact & Support
- **Contact Us**: Navigate to the Contact page to send a query to the support team.
- **Chatbot**: content the AI chatbot icon in the bottom right corner to ask quick questions about the platform or EVs.

### 1.3 Registration
- Click **"Login"** -> **"Register"**.
- Choose your role:
    - **User (Buyer)**: For purchasing vehicles and accessories.
    - **Seller**: For listing vehicles and managing sales.
- Fill in your details (Name, Email, Password) and click Register.

## 2. Buyer Guide
*For customers looking to buy EVs, book test drives, or seek financing.*

### 2.1 Dashboard
- Access your personal **Dashboard** to see an overview of your Recent Orders, Wishlist items, and Profile status.

### 2.2 Buying a Vehicle
1. **Search**: Go to the **Models** page and click on a vehicle card.
2. **View Details**: detailed specs, battery health, and images.
3. **Add to Cart**: Click **"Add to Cart"** to save it for purchase.
4. **Checkout**:
    - Go to your Cart (or click Checkout).
    - Enter your **Shipping Address**.
    - Enter valid **Credit Card** details.
    - Click **"Pay"** to complete the order.
5. **Order History**: Track your order status (Pending, Shipped, Delivered) in the **Orders** tab.

### 2.3 Test Drives
- On any Vehicle Detail page, click **"Book Test Drive"**.
- Select a valid **future date** and time slot.
- View and manage your appointments in the **Test Drive** section of your Dashboard.

### 2.4 Financing
- Navigate to the **Financing** tab in your Dashboard.
- Click **"Apply for Financing"**.
- Fill out the form regarding your income and employment.
- Upload necessary documents (PDF/Images).
- Submit the application and await approval from a Financial Officer.

### 2.5 Community & Reviews
- **Community**: Join discussions, create posts, and reply to other EV enthusiasts.
- **Reviews**: After purchasing a vehicle, go to your Order History to leave a star rating and review for the product.

## 3. Seller Guide
*For dealerships or individuals listing EVs for sale.*

### 3.1 Dashboard & Inventory
- **Overview**: View total Sales, Revenue, and Inventory count.
- **Add Vehicle**:
    - Click **"Add Vehicle"** button.
    - **Step 1**: Select Brand and Category.
    - **Step 2**: Enter Model Name, Year, and Specifications (Range, Speed, etc.).
    - **Step 3**: Set Price, Condition, and Battery Health.
    - **Step 4**: Upload high-quality metadata (Max 5).
    - Click **"Submit"** to list the vehicle.
- **Edit/Delete**: Managing your listings directly from the Inventory table.

### 3.2 Order Management
- Go to the **Orders** tab.
- View new orders from buyers.
- Update status from **"Pending"** to **"Shipped"** or **"Delivered"** as you process them.

### 3.3 Test Drive Requests
- Monitor incoming Test Drive requests in the **Test Drives** tab.
- **Approve** or **Reject** requests based on vehicle availability.

### 3.4 Repair Locations
- If you have service centers, add them in the **Repair Locations** tab.
- Enter the address and coordinates to have them appear on the user's service map.

## 4. Financial Officer Guide
*For bank/finance staff managing loan applications.*

### 4.1 Dashboard
- View a high-level summary of **Pending Applications** and active loans.

### 4.2 Application Processing
- Navigate to the **Applications** list.
- Review Buyer submissions (Income details, documents).
- **Approve**: Grants the financing request; the buyer is notified.
- **Reject**: Denies the request (optional reason).

### 4.3 Loan Products
- Manage (Add/Edit) available loan schemes (Interest rates, Tenure options) that Buyers can choose from.

---

## 5. Administrator Guide
*For platform owners managing users and system health.*

### 5.1 User Management
- **Users**: View a list of all Buyers and Sellers.
- **Ban/Unban**: Restrict access for suspicious or malicious accounts.

### 5.2 Content Moderation
- **Community**: Monitor user posts. Delete inappropriate content to maintain community standards.
- **Complaints**: Review complaints submitted by users and mark them as Resolved.

### 5.3 Seller Approval
- Review new Seller account requests.
- Verify their business details before granting them access to list vehicles.

### 5.4 System & AI
- **ML Pipeline**: Trigger retraining of the Price Prediction or Battery Health AI models if new data is available.
- **Settings**: Toggle global system settings (e.g., Maintenance Mode).
